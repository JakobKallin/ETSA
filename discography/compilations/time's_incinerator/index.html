Trivia

  • Contains B-sides, outtakes, demos, live performances, and other rarities of early Soul Asylum/Loud Fast Rules material.
  • Is the rarest official Soul Asylum album, partially due to it being a cassette-only release.
  • The album cover photo was taken by Dave.
  • The album name is taken from Can't Go Back. The lyrics are “fifteen years later caught in time's incinerator, yesterday's worries are today's”.
