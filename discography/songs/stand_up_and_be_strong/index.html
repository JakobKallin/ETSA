Trivia

  • Was used by ABC and ESPN for the 2006 college football season telecasts.
  • Is usually one, if not the last song when performed live.
  • When Dave sings "You might have to fight", the audience follows shortly after shouting "fight".
  • Dave usually thanks the crowd for coming to the concert, being a "dynamite audience", and to give yourself a round of applause.
