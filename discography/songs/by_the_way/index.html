Trivia

  • The studio version first appeared on WKZQ New York radio (accompanying an interview with Dave).
  • The original By the Way was actually a studio demo during the Grave Dancers Union sessions and made its way onto b-sides of singles and compilations before having a studio album recording (on Delayed Reaction) almost 20 years later.

Appearances

  • On the album Delayed Reaction.
  • On the compilation Playlist: The Very Best of Soul Asylum.
  • On the Runaway Train single.
  • On the Somebody To Shove single.
