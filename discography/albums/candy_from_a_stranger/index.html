Candy From A Stranger

Release Date May 12th, 1998
Producer Chris Kimsey
Label Columbia
Formats CD, cassette, vinyl
Length 45:11

Tracklist

  1. Creatures of Habit
  2. I Will Still Be Laughing
  3. Close
  4. See You Later
  5. No Time For Waiting
  6. Blood Into Wine
  7. Lies of Hate
  8. Draggin' the Lake
  9. New York Blackout
  10. The Game
  11. Cradle Chain
  12. Losin' It (Japanese version only)

Trivia

  • Was going to include the song Candy From A Stranger, but it was cut from the final release. It was soon released on a limited edition CD and later on Black Gold.
  • Was marketed in part through the distribution of chocolate bars (resembling Hershey's) labeled “Candy From A Stranger”.

Singles

  1. I Will Still Be Laughing
  2. Close

Charts

  • Peaked at #121 on the Billboard 200 chart.
  • Peaked at #27 on the Billboard Modern Rock chart.
