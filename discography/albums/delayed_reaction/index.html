Delayed Reaction

Release Date July 17th, 2012
Producer Soul Asylum overseen by John Fields
Label 429 Records
Formats CD, vinyl
Length 35:41

Tracklist

  1. Gravity
  2. Into the Light (Breaking Horses)
  3. The Streets
  4. By the Way
  5. Pipe Dream
  6. Let's All Kill Each Other
  7. Cruel Intentions
  8. The Juice
  9. Take Manhattan
  10. I Should Have Stayed In Bed
  11. Leave This Town (Best Buy only)
  12. Time Will Tell (Best Buy only)
  13. Your Generation (Best Buy only)
  14. Good Morning Good Morning (Best Buy only)

Trivia

  • Rough Air was originally considered as the title of this album.
  • Is the first Soul Asylum album to have a Parental Advisory label.
  • Its cover features the motif The Dowry, an oil on linen painting from 2000 by Bo Bartlett.
  • Was also released as a Best Buy exclusive that included the songs Good Morning Good Morning, Your Generation, Leave This Town, and Time Will Tell.
  • Was officially announced on March 26th, 2012.
