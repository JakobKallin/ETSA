Quiz: Trains and Phones

It has been said that Soul Asylum's lyrics are abundant with references to trains and phones. Here is a quiz about those very lyrics!

  1. In Bitter Pill, the narrator sees his train coming, but something is wrong with it. What?
  2. In Broken Glass, when the narrator calls a 608 number, what does he hear at the other end?
  3. In Closer To the Stars, who is climbing up the phone pole?
  4. In Easy Street, does the friend pick up the phone?
  5. In Good For You, where is the train headed?
  6. In Nice Guys (Don't Get Paid), what is the train's passenger car filled with?
  7. In Passing Sad Daydream, what does the narrator do to his subject's phone?
  8. In Runaway Train, when does the narrator call his friend up?
  9. In String of Pearls, who takes the train to the funeral?
  10. In Tell Me When, what does the woman do with her phone?
  11. Which of the these songs does not have a train or phone reference in it?
