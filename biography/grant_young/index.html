Grant Young

Full Name Grant D. Young
Birthdate January 5, 1964 (51 years)
Birthplace Iowa City, Iowa
Position Drummer, backup vocalist
Instruments played Yamaha drums with Zildjian cymbals
Duration in band 1985–1994

Trivia

  • Shortly after leaving the band he started up a vacation resort in northeastern Minnesota; it has since been sold.
  • Although he played them live on tour, he only performed on about half of the songs on Grave Dancers Union. One of the songs he didn't perform was Runaway Train.
  • Has consumed 23 powdered donuts in one sitting.
